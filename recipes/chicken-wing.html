Chicken Wing

How to make great chicken wings

The simplest chicken wings are healthy, tasty, and savory unless you're a fatty animal who likes to drown in some sugar sauce. Pure blasphemy. Very few meat should be sweet. Here's how you make dem wings:

Ingredients

  • chicken wings, as much as you want
  • salt, can be replaced with soy sauce
  • pepper
  1. Marinate chicken wings with salt (or soy sauce) and pepper until even
  2. Place wings in an air fryer try flat, with the skin side down
  3. Air fry at 400 degrees for 10 minutes
  4. Flip wings so skins side up, air fry at 400 degrees for 10 minutes
