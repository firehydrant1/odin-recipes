How to make great Pesto Salmons

Literally, buy it at Costco. This thing here is fantastic and simple with low effort requirements. Find the consistency you like after a few times. Here is mine

Ingredients

  • Pesto Salmons from Costco
  • lemon
  1. Preheat oven to 450
  2. Remove lid and stick into top tray of oven for 20 minutes
  3. Broil for 3 minutes
